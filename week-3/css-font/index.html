Generic font family

In css,there are five generic font family


  1. font family:seref fonts have a small stroke at the edge of each letter and they are create a sense of formality and elegant
  2. sound-seref:This fonts are clean line,(no small stroke attached) They create a modern and minimalistic look
  3. Mono-space:This fonts have all their letters with the same fixed width.They create a mechanical look
  4. Cursive-font:This font imitate human handwritting
  5. Fantasy-font:This are decorative/playful fonts

All the different font-mates belongs to one of the generic family in design,font family are referred to as type phase
